Who are we?

We are a group of pickleball fanatics. Probably just like you. Maybe a little nerdier. We love statistics, and seeing realtime feedback on our play. Our leagues aim to honor this love and balance it with a healthy dose of friendly competition. We think our league formats are some of the most creative, most fun you'll have on the pickleball court. We hope you agree. Welcome to PALMS, an oasis of fun.
